# PathAI - Learn Languages Smarter

## What is PathAI?

PathAI is your personal AI-powered language learning companion. Our intelligent system creates personalized learning paths tailored to your level, helping you build vocabulary and master grammar naturally.

## How It Works

1. **Get Started** - Set your language level and learning goals
2. **Practice Smarter** - Access AI-generated exercises and vocabulary lessons personalized for you
3. **Track Progress** - Build your learning streaks and watch your vocabulary grow
4. **Keep Learning** - Consistent daily practice with adaptive difficulty

## Key Features

- **Personalized Lessons** - AI-generated exercises tailored to your level and pace
- **Smart Vocabulary Building** - Learn words and phrases that matter for your goals
- **Learning Streaks** - Stay motivated with daily learning goals
- **Progress Tracking** - See exactly how much you've learned

## Why PathAI?

Instead of boring flashcards or generic lessons, you get a learning experience that adapts to *you*. Our AI handles the heavy lifting—figuring out what you need to learn next—so you can spend time actually absorbing the language naturally.

**Start learning today. No setup. No downloads. Just watch, learn, grow.**
